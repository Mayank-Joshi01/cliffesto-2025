ABOUT US

Welcome to Cliffesto 2025, where innovation meets celebration! Step into the vibrant world of NIT's annual techno-cultural fest, a one-of-a-kind extravaganza that bridges the realms of technology, creativity, and culture. Cliffesto is more than just an event—it's a symphony of ideas, talent, and passion, designed to ignite the spark of imagination and innovation in every participant. With electrifying competitions, enriching workshops, mesmerizing cultural performances, and inspiring sessions by thought leaders, Cliffesto is the ultimate confluence of technology and art. Join us in this grand celebration, where the boundaries between tech and culture dissolve, and the future of creativity comes to life. Celebrate the spirit of innovation and culture at Cliffesto 2025!

EVENTS

Get ready to experience the best of both worlds at Cliffesto 2025, our college's annual techno-cultural fest! Prepare for an unforgettable fusion of innovation, creativity, and culture, with events like Cadathon, where designs come to life; the thrilling FPV Drone Race, a spectacle of skill and speed; and a captivating Treasure Hunt that'll challenge your wit and instincts. But that's not all—Cliffesto brings a cultural twist with breathtaking performances, engaging workshops, and interactive showcases that celebrate the diversity of talent. Whether you're a tech wizard, a creative soul, or someone ready to soak in the fest's vibrant energy, Cliffesto 2025 has something for everyone. Join us for an electrifying journey where technology and culture collide in the most spectacular way.
Cliffesto 2025: Where innovation and culture take the stage!

CULTURAL EVENTS Our fest's cultural events feature dance, music, drama, fashion shows, skits, poetry, debates, storytelling, art, comedy, and traditional performances.

LITERARY EVENTS Our fest's literary events include Kavi Sammelan, debate, quiz, poetry, storytelling, essay writing, elocution, creative writing, and book discussions.

TECHNICAL EVENTS Our fest's technical events include coding, robotics, hackathons, AI challenges, quizzes, tech debates, workshops, circuit design, gaming, and innovation expos.
